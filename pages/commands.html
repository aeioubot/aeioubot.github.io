Fun Commands

ping: Checks the bot's ping to the Discord server. sparkle: Makes your words sparkly: wizard: A wizard says your words using his special wizard powers. ask: Grabs a random question from Ask Reddit.

Miscellaneous Commands

begin: Contains information on how to begin the various games Aeiou has. invite: Gives an invite link to add Aeiou to your server. shards: Shows data for all shards. support: DMs you a link to the support server.

Utility

help: Displays a list of available commands, or detailed information for a specified command. prefix: Shows or sets the command prefix.
